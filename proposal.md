## What are you building? Who is it for?
I am building a landing page for Pursuit of Happiness Productions, and it will be an extremely 
immersive portfolio that displays all of the work that this company has done and will allow me to land more clients. 

## Why? What problem does it solve?
This company is selling storytelling videos, so their website needs to be able to do that. 
It removes so much of the work that I have to do on my end in order to continue to put together 
specific briefs for each client and send them samples of my work. Everything will live on this site. 

MVP: Bio, work portfolio/embeds, contact
Stretch: Animated sections, TikTok feed integration, client inquiry form, case studies
